WebScraped Job Market Insights

Data-driven insights from Indian job market using web scraping, EDA, and visual analytics.

📌 Project Overview

This project aims to uncover hiring trends, skill demands, job categories, and city-level patterns in the Indian job market. Using Python-based web scraping techniques, we extracted job data and performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) and visualizations to identify actionable insights for aspiring Data Analysts.

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Python

Selenium (Web Scraping)

Pandas (Data Wrangling)

Matplotlib / Seaborn (Visualizations)

Jupyter Notebook

📊 Visuals & Insights

1. Top Hiring Companies by Experience Level

Shows which companies are hiring across Fresher, Entry, Mid, and Senior roles.

2. Job Category Distribution (Pie Chart)

Breakdown of job demand by experience level — Fresher, Entry, etc.

3. Top 10 Hiring Cities

City-wise distribution of job postings. Metro cities lead.

4. Top 10 In-Demand Skills

Visual analysis of most commonly required skills (SQL, Excel, Python, Power BI).

5. Top Job Titles (Log Scale)

Frequency of titles like Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Business Analyst.

📈 Key Takeaways

✅ Wipro and other service-based firms dominate hiring across all levels.

✅ Most demand is for Fresher and Entry-Level roles.

✅ SQL, Excel, Python, and Power BI are top required skills.

✅ Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Mumbai are top job markets.
